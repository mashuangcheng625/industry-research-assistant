# Frontend acceptance contract

## Product

Present one semiconductor industry research assistant with four peer research directions: chip design, semiconductor materials and equipment, semiconductor front-end manufacturing, and advanced packaging.

## Preserve

- Authentication and API request behavior.
- Existing chat, upload, knowledge-base, memory, database, and news flows.
- Existing route compatibility unless a route is demonstrably broken.

## Visual direction

Use a credible professional research-tool aesthetic: restrained technical blue/cyan accents, clear information density, strong evidence/source affordances, and consistent design tokens. Avoid generic consumer-chat styling and decorative “AI glow” that competes with research content.

## Required viewports

- 1440 × 900 desktop
- 1024 × 768 tablet
- 390 × 844 mobile

## Quality gate

No blocking issue; total rubric score at least 8.5/10; every applicable dimension at least 8.0/10.
